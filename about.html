About Us

Brand History

Tate’s Confectionery House was established on the 10th of January 2018. I started the company and I assumed full responsibility for the execution of success of TCH. Baking is my passion. I got it from my mother a professional baker 20 years ago though not well known.

I only perfected when I grew up. Hence it was a no brainer for me. I realised I didn’t just want a job for job’s sake. I did not want to work for under –appreciative bosses and I did not want to spend the rest of my life doing what I did not remotely enjoy. I wanted to create, I wanted to bake, I wanted to be a boss.
